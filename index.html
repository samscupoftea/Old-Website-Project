About me

I was born and grew up in a northern seaside town called Cleethorpe. I never imagined that my life be filled with incredible experience and that it would take me all over the world. Below are some key highlights.

Teaching & Travelling

I lived abroad for six years and taught english in countries such as Cambodia, Thailand and South Korea.

Music

I am singer-songwriter. My main instrument is guitar and my songs have been featured nationally on various BBC radio stations.

Martial Arts

I have practiced Brazillian Jiu Jitsu since 2018 and was awarded blue belt from Master Park (The first black belt in South Korea). I also competed twice in the BJJ Busan open.
